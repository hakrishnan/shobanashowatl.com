The Show

Krishna - The Musical

Dwell in the magic of ...

“Krishna” - an enchanting musical Directed and Choreographed  by Padma Shri and National Award winning Danseuse/leading South Indian Actor “Shobhana”.

The musical showcases "Krishna" - The man, his philosophy and why he is still loved beyond man’s understanding,all on a canvas of Vrindavan and Mathura to Kurukshetra

An experience never to be missed. Watch it live for the best experience

the first indian musical, in English

The design of Krishna does not follow the conventional patterns. Instead, it is a symphony of various dance forms ranging from ancient classical forms to Bollywood film dances. Though the story of Krishna has been told in several languages, this is the first full-fledged song and dance performance on Krishna in English.
